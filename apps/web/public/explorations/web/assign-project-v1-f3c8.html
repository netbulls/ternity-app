Design Notes — V1: The Member List

Approach: A straightforward list-based member management panel within a project settings page. An "Add Members" button reveals an inline search panel above the list.

  • Pro: Familiar list pattern — minimal learning curve. Users immediately see who is assigned.
  • Pro: Search-to-add is inline, no modal interruption. Fast for adding one or two people.
  • Pro: Role badges are visible at a glance. Member count in the header gives quick context.
  • Con: Can get long for large teams — no grouping or filtering of existing members.
  • Con: No bulk operations — each member must be removed individually.
  • Con: Role changes would need an additional interaction (not shown: dropdown on badge click).
