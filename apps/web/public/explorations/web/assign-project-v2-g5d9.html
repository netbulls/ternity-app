Design Notes — V2: The Avatar Grid

Approach: Visual avatar grid where assigned members have teal ring borders and unassigned are dimmed. Click to toggle assignment. A role indicator dot distinguishes managers from users. Toggle between grid and detailed list view.

  • Pro: Highly visual — great for teams where people recognize each other by initials/photos. Fast scanning.
  • Pro: Toggle-to-assign is extremely fast for bulk changes. One click per person.
  • Pro: Role dots provide subtle but clear hierarchy info. Teal ring = assigned is immediately obvious.
  • Pro: Dual view (grid + list) gives flexibility — grid for quick overview, list for detailed control.
  • Con: Less information density than a list — no email visible in grid mode.
  • Con: Scales poorly beyond ~20 people — grid becomes overwhelming.
  • Con: Toggle-to-assign could cause accidental removals if clicked carelessly.
