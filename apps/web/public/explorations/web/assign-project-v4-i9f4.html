Design Notes — The Kanban Swim

Pros

  • Role distinction is immediately visible — lane = role
  • Gamified feel makes bulk assignment less tedious
  • No modal or dropdown friction — direct manipulation
  • Visual card density shows team size at a glance
  • Search across all lanes unifies the experience
  • Horizontal cards maximize info density per lane

Cons

  • Drag & drop can be fiddly on mobile / touchscreens
  • Scales poorly beyond ~30 users (lanes get very long)
  • No bulk operations — one card at a time
  • Horizontal scrolling may be needed for many cards
  • Users unfamiliar with kanban may not intuit the drag mechanic
  • Accessibility concerns with drag-based interfaces
